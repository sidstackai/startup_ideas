# Idea 6: HighlightHero (Amateur Sports Analytics)
1. The Core Concept
A "Broadcast-as-a-Service" platform for amateur sports turfs. It uses computer vision to turn a Sunday match into a pro-level highlights package with heatmaps and stats.

2. Target Market & ICP
Segment: Urban Turf Owners and "Serious-Amateur" players.

Geography: Tier-1 cities with high football/box-cricket density.

User Persona: The "Weekend Warrior"—millennials who spend on premium turf, boots, and kits, and are obsessed with their digital presence (Instagram/Strava).

3. The Problem & JTBD
The JTBD: "Record my best moments so I can share my 'Glory Days' with my friends and track my fitness progress."

The Pain: The "Ghost Goal." You score the goal of your life, but no one saw it, and there’s no video.

The Gap: Professional analytics (Veo/Catapult) cost thousands of dollars and require manual setup.

4. The Solution Architecture
The Vision Rig: A weather-proof, 4K triple-camera array permanently mounted at the turf.

The Auto-Clipper: AI that detects "high-energy events" (goals, wickets, saves) and automatically creates 15-second vertical "Reels" with slow-mo and music.

The Ego-Dashboard: Each player gets a "Profile" with distance covered, top speed, and "Win Rate" across different turfs.

5. "The Enemy"
Primary Enemy: The shaky, low-res smartphone recording by a friend on the sidelines.

Secondary Enemy: The "Forgotten Effort"—spending 60 mins playing but having zero data to show for it.

6. GTM Strategy (Siddharth’s Leverage)
The Wedge: Offer the hardware for free to the top 10 turfs in Bengaluru in exchange for a revenue share on the "Premium Reels."

The Channel: Social-loop. Every reel shared on Instagram includes a "Play at this Turf" CTA, driving more bookings for the owner.

7. Business Model & Scale
Pricing: ₹99 per match for "Personal Highlights" or ₹499 for "Team Full-Stats Package."

Potential: 5,000 turfs in India x ₹20,000/month rev-share = ₹120 Cr ($14M) ARR.
