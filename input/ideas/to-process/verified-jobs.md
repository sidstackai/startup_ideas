# Idea 3: ProofPoint (The Verified Talent Marketplace)
1. The Core Concept
A "Simulation-First" hiring platform that replaces the CV with immutable Proof-of-Work. It moves hiring from "Read-Only" (scanning resumes) to "Play-Back" (watching a candidate solve real-world problems).

2. Target Market & ICP
Segment: High-growth Series A to Series C Tech Startups.

Geography: Global Remote (India/SEA talent to US/EU companies).

User Persona: The "Burnt-Out Recruiter" or Engineering Manager who is tired of interviewing candidates who look great on LinkedIn but can't think logically in a live environment.

3. The Problem & JTBD
The JTBD: "Give me 3 candidates who I know can do the job so I don't waste 40 hours of my engineering team's time on bad interviews."

The Pain: AI-generated resume spam has made traditional filters useless. Identity and skill fraud in remote hiring is at an all-time high.

The Gap: LinkedIn is a "social network" for bragging; it isn't a "verification network" for competence.

4. The Solution Architecture
The Sandbox Environment: An in-browser IDE or PM-Dashboard where candidates solve a "Staged Crisis" (e.g., "Fix this production bug" or "Draft a PRD for this churning feature").

The Behavioral AI Proctor: Tracks logic-flow, screen-switches, and "Time-to-Insight" to ensure no external AI (ChatGPT) was used to cheat.

The Skill Passport: An on-chain, immutable record of the candidate's performance that they can carry to any employer.

5. "The Enemy"
Primary Enemy: The "Resume Optimization" Industry (bots and services that help bad candidates look good).

Secondary Enemy: The "Vibe-Check" Interview—where hiring happens based on personality rather than verified skill.

6. GTM Strategy (Siddharth’s Leverage)
The Wedge: Target PES University and other Tier-1/2 colleges. Give students a "ProofPoint Score" to help them bypass the resume-screening hell for top startups.

The Channel: Using CXO network to sell "Trial-as-a-Service" to hungry startups looking for "zero-friction" hiring.

7. Business Model & Scale
Pricing: $1,000 per successful hire + SaaS fee for the simulation platform.

Potential: 1% of the 1.5M global tech hires annually = $150M+ ARR potential.
